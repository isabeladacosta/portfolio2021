UX/UI Design

As a UX/UI Designer I care about the users needs in order to create intuitive and sustainable digital products focused on the user experience.
The following projects were done within universty context. I had to create the usability and interface for three different apps with three different types of users.

The first project, breadfast.club, was aimed to families and young couples that want a digital service for breakfast delivery which cares about quality and sustainable products for their first meal of the day.
The second one, sesh safety, an app to bring awareness to harm reduction. had young adults on focus.
The last one, was a social network for female djs and producers to promote their work and create a community of support for and networking for like minded artists.
